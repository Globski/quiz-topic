# Question: What are the top 10 best websites to download high-quality, free stock photos for personal or commercial use?

> * **Answer:**
>   Here are the top 10 free stock photo websites:

1. **#10 Free Range** – Offers thousands of images from in-house and external photographers; requires an account to download.
2. **#9 Freepik** – Provides photos, vectors, PSD files, and icons; some images require attribution.
3. **#8 Stock Vault** – Features over 140,000 curated photos with helpful category search options.
4. **#7 Burst** – Created by Shopify, ideal for entrepreneurs needing images for business marketing.
5. **#6 MorgueFile** – One of the oldest platforms (founded in 1996); attribution required if images are not modified.
6. **#5 Adobe Stock Free Collection** – Offers free photos, videos, and vectors without needing a paid subscription.
7. **#4 StockSnap** – Adds hundreds of new high-quality photos weekly.
8. **#3 Unsplash** – Hosts over 3 million curated photos contributed by photographers worldwide.
9. **#2 Pexels** – Popular for high-quality images and a strong collection of free stock videos.
10. **#1 Pixabay** – The top choice with over 5 million photos, vectors, videos, and illustrations.

**Honorable Mentions:**

* Wikimedia Commons – Over 128 million freely usable media files.
* Vecteezy – Extensive library of vectors, PNGs, and stock videos.
* Kaboom Pics – High-quality images with a simple and user-friendly license.
