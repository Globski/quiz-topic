# Question: Why is UI/UX design important in modern product development?

**Answer:** Because the success of digital products depends heavily on how users interact with them.

---

# Question: What types of products rely heavily on UI/UX design?

**Answer:** Mobile applications, websites, and enterprise platforms.

---

# Question: What do organizations aim to improve by prioritizing user experience?

**Answer:** Customer satisfaction, engagement, and business outcomes.

---

# Question: What are the design topics?

**Answer:** Wireframing, prototyping, design systems, usability principles, and real-world projects, Design thinking, user psychology, and decision-making processes.
